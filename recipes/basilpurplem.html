Purple Basil Lemonade

    - Handful of purple basil
    - Batch of lemonade

    An awesome enhancement to the summertime classic. Take a handful of purple basil and blend it with a cup or so of your favorite lemonade -- we use a stick blender in a mason jar for this. Strain the basil pulp out of the puree and into your batch of lemonade. Enjoy with a spring of basil, and perhaps a little gin!
