Purple Basil Lemonade

    • 1 Handful of purple basil
    • 1 Batch of lemonade

    An awesome enhancement to the summertime classic. Take a handful of purple basil and blend it with a cup or so of your favorite lemonade -- we use a stick blender in a mason jar for this. Strain the basil pulp out of the puree and into your batch of lemonade.

    Enjoy with a sprig of basil, and perhaps a little gin!
